About Us

Welcome to the Magdi Yacoub Foundation, where healthcare meets compassion, and every heartbeat counts. Founded in 2008 by the eminent cardiac surgeon, Prof. Magdi Yacoub, our foundation is a beacon of hope and healing, dedicated to making quality healthcare accessible to all.

❮ ❯

Our Journey

The journey began with a visionary idea — to bring world-class healthcare to those who need it most. Over the years, we have evolved, expanded, and made a lasting impact on countless lives. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a global force for positive change in the field of healthcare.

Our Mission

At the Magdi Yacoub Foundation, we are driven by a singular mission: to transform lives through accessible and compassionate healthcare. Founded in 2008 by renowned cardiac surgeon Prof. Magdi Yacoub, our foundation is committed to alleviating suffering, fostering innovation, and creating sustainable healthcare solutions for communities in need.
